About

Our Vision

To provide the residents of the Greater Vancouver Area with opportunities to investigate volunteering opportunities easily and safely.

Our Mission

LendAHand is a tool for non-profit organizations dedicated to decreasing barriers to entry into volunteerism. Lend a hand strives to provide its classified services to all non-profit organizations in the Greater Vancouver Area, regardless of NPO capacity or budget.
