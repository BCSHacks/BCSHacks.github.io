Food Prep

Food Prep

Weekly
BC Children's Hospital
Vancouver

Under the guidance and supervision of the kitchen manager, volunteers will help prepare, cook, and bake food to be served in the cafe. A typical shift involves:

  • Chopping veggies and mixing up spices for our stews
  • Baking delicious breads and baked goods
  • Cleaning up the kitchen after cooking with the team

If you are interested in experiencing what it is like to cook in an industrial kitchen, meeting other foodies, and developing your kitchen skills, come lend a hand in our kitchen!

No experience is necessary, as our kitchen managers and assistants will be on-site to share their knowledge and provide assistance.

Volunteers are required to commit to weekly 2-3 hour shifts. Choose between the hours of:

  • Sundays from 5 PM to 8 PM or
  • Tuesdays from 5 PM to 8 PM
